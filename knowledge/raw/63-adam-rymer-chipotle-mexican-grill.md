# Adam Rymer

|     |
| --- |
| ![](https://ir.chipotle.com/image/HeadshotsOnWood_Management_AdamRymer-320x320p.jpg) |

Chief Financial Officer

**Adam Rymer** is the Chief Financial Officer for Chipotle Mexican Grill, responsible for corporate and field financial planning and analysis, as well as the financial reporting, tax, treasury and investor relations functions. Mr. Rymer is a member of the Executive Leadership Team, reporting to the Chief Executive Officer. He previously served as Vice President of Finance and has held a variety of positions in Finance and People Experience since joining the organization in 2009.  Additionally, he is the Treasurer of the Chipotle Cultivate Foundation. In 2025, Mr. Rymer was named Extel All America Best CFO for the restaurant sector.

Prior to Chipotle, Mr. Rymer held corporate finance positions at Rock Bottom Restaurants and Travelocity and was also a Which Wich franchisee.

Mr. Rymer has a Bachelor of Business Administration degree in finance from the University of Texas at Austin.
