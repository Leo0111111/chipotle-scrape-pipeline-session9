## Business Description

Chipotle operates Mexican-inspired fast-casual restaurants offering burritos, bowls, quesadillas, tacos, and salads. The company emphasizes responsibly sourced proteins (chicken, beef, pork) and provides digital ordering via website, mobile app, and third-party delivery platforms across the U.S., Canada, France, and other markets.
